ABOUT ME

As a Marketing Communications Officer at Mac Partners Training Institute (MPTI), I develop and execute comprehensive marketing communication strategies that enhance corporate branding and accelerate growth and profitability. I create high-quality content and engaging marketing collateral, using my strong graphic and digital skills, excellent communication abilities, and passion for marketing.

I also have valuable experience in video production, drone piloting, and digital promotions, as the Creative Team Lead for the Quality 4 Water initiative in Ghana and the Video Production Manager for Caleb Kudah's YouTube channel. I spearhead the development and implementation of captivating and top-notch video content, promotional tactics, and public relations initiatives, aimed at highlighting the significance of water quality and conservation, and delivering educational and entertaining content to a large audience.

I hold a Master of Arts in Brands and Communications Management from the University of Professional Studies, Accra, a Bachelor of Arts in Communication Design from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Kumasi and 50+ Online Certifications. I am eager to explore opportunities in various corporate sectors, including mining, oil and gas, manufacturing and education, where I can apply my creative and innovative skills to create positive change and serve my community and country in meaningful ways.
